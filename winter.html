The Winter Season

Depending where you are, you many not get a winter. Winter is the season where it gets cold and everything freezes, in some places. It is the coldest season of the four, expect short days and long nights.

What to expect

Depending where you are, if you regularly get winters, stay bundled up. Winters differ state to state. Some colder then others, and some not cold at all, like Texas.
