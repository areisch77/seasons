The Autumn Season

Autumn is probably one of the most beautiful times of the year. It is also a very colorful time of the year too. All the leaves are changing colors and falling off the trees. It makes the tranisition from summer to winter.

Autumn Expectations

Expect some weather change, it will start to cool down a bit because of the transition to the winter season. Daylight becomes noticably shorter. Autumn traditionally starts September 21st. The main thing this season is none for is the sheading of its leaves.
