The Sunny Spring Season

Spring is the season most people look forward to after a long winter. People refer to spring as the season of new beginnings. Its a sign that winter is over. It is also a exciting time for the farmers, as they plant their seeds and start farmer when springtime hits.

Celebration of Spring

Many cultures celebrate in mid March when spring hits, they refer to it as he rise of the vernal equinox. In japan, they have a annuel blossum ceremony to watch their cherry trees blossum in the spring. They gather in parks and shrines to celebrate the new season.
